Regional Assessment Insights

Agricultural Property Valuation

Agricultural properties in Southeastern Washington are assessed based on their current use value, not market value. This includes consideration of water rights, soil quality, and crop yields over a 5-year period.

Vineyard and Winery Valuations

The unique vineyard and winery properties in Walla Walla and Benton counties require specialized assessment methodologies that account for both agricultural and commercial components.

Hanford Area Special Considerations

Properties in North Richland and other areas near the Hanford Site may have special assessment considerations due to their proximity to federal lands and the unique employment market.

Water Rights Valuation

Water rights in Southeastern Washington are a critical component of property value, particularly for agricultural land. Proper documentation and valuation of these rights is essential for accurate assessment.
